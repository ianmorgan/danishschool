h1. Practical information 


The "Danish School Down Under" meets on most Sundays during term time. We currently have 3 classes offering different levels from beginner to advanced. 

The hours are 10.00 am to 12.30pm (with a short break).

The classes are held at the 
"Richmond Primary School":http://www.richmondps.vic.edu.au/contact-us, Mary Street, Richmond.
